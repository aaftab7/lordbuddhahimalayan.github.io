Delivery time & process

The entire time frame for order delivery is divided into two aspects:

Processing time : The time it takes to prepare your item(s) to ship from our warehouse. This includes preparing your items, performing quality checks, and packing for shipment. Shipping Time : The time for your item(s) to travel from our warehouse to your destination. Please note: Total Delivery Time = Processing Time + Shipping Time

PROCESSING At Creative Hand Nepal we value each and every single customer. Our top priority is making sure the items get delivered to our customer as soon as possible. It normally takes 15-25 business days for us to process your order from the date that it is placed. Please note that holidays & weekends are not included.

Fast shipment

If your shipment is very urgent, you can use some express courier services like FEDEX, DHL, UPS, TNT or ARAMEX This is the fastest way to ship goods, door-to-door, but is quiet expensive. It's also the solution we generally use for jewelry order.

Air Cargo

Air Freight offers an alternative for shipping items weighing 70 kg and above and measured more than 1.5 meters in length. Price wise, it is much more economical than Dhl, Fedex, Tnt, Skynet or UPS. items above 70kg. However, It is slower than Dhl, Fedex, Tnt, Skynet or UPS services, normally it's take a 7 to 12 days from Nepal to reach most of the countries in the world . But you can send combination orders with numerous bulky items, which make it impossible or too costly to send either by Dhl, Fedex, Tnt, Skynet or UPS. The buyer pays per kegs or per volumetric kegs. (Depends which one is the heaviest). Minimum 70 kilos.

Note: Shipping can be organized through various air lines. The goods has to be picked by you only from your nearest air port and you yourself has to handle the custom procedures.
